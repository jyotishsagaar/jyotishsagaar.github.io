## 🚀 HOW TO USE

### **1. View the Website**
Simply open `index.html` in your web browser to see the full professional design.

### **2. Add Your Images**
Place your images in the `images/` folder:
- `logo.jpeg` - Your logo (circular)
- `founder.jpeg` - Your founder photo
- `banner.png` - Hero section background

### **3. Customize Text**
Edit `index.html` to update:
- Contact details
- Social media links
- Testimonials
- About section content

### **4. Modify Colors** (Optional)
Edit `style.css` to change:
- Gold color: `#ffd700` → your color
- Dark background: `#030810` → your color
- Any other colors as needed
